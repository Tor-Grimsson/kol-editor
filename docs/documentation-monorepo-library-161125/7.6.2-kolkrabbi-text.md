## Home / About Narrative Copy

### Kolkrabbi Vinnustofa (About Section)

Kolkrabbi is a design studio and atelier in Reykjavík focused on visual identities, branding, art direction, and design systems. Whether a sleek website, a meaningful visual identity, or a specific user experience is needed, the studio brings the vision to life—taking time to understand scope, roadmap, and deliverables so projects land on schedule.

### Studio Variations

**Version 1**
> Kolkrabbi, founded in 2019, based in Reykjavík, Iceland and home to artist and designer Tór Grímsson, is a full-service design studio and atelier focused on brand identity, UI/UX and traditional graphic design for clients of all sizes.  
> The studio creates customised solutions for brands looking to differentiate their visual presence.

**Version 2**
> Founded in 2019, Kolkrabbi is a creative design studio and atelier based in Reykjavík, Iceland. Home of artist and designer Tór Grímsson.  
> A solution-based full-service design studio focused on brand identity, UI/UX and traditional graphic design. The studio creates customised solutions tailored to project specifics through brand building, art direction, illustration, UI/UX, web development, and consultation. Kolkrabbi partners with other experienced professionals to expand capabilities as needed.

**Version 3**
> Kolkrabbi is a design studio and atelier located in Reykjavík, founded by Tór Grímsson in 2019 to encompass project-based freelance work.  
> Centred around client work, Kolkrabbi creates customised solutions tailored to project specifics through brand building, art direction, and consultation.  
> Offerings span brand design, typography, market research, digital design, UI/UX, illustration, print systems, and production-ready assets.

### Tór Guðmundsson / Tór Grímsson Bio

**Short Version**  
Tór (Þórður, phonetically Tór-tór) is the creative director of the studio. Working between digital and physical design, Tór’s output ranges from pen-and-paper studies to 3D work. Personal projects appear in exhibitions and galleries under aliases such as Svartval, Biskup, and Konsulat.

**Full Version**  
Tór is an artist, designer, producer, and organiser focused on visual identity, design systems, and illustration. He holds degrees from the Icelandic University of the Arts and Weissensee KHS in Berlin, plus art theory studies at the University of Iceland. With 15+ years in the creative industry—fine arts, performances, film, editorial, identities, UI/UX, and systems—each discipline informs the next. Kolkrabbi is headquartered in Reykjavík, where Tór creates value through art direction, branding, consultation, and experimentation. Outside the studio he produces artwork as Biskup and music as Konsulat.
