## Process

### Three-Phase Approach

**01. Discover & Define Phase**
This stage focuses on understanding the design problem, aligning with the brand's core values, and identifying unique differentiators.

**02. Design Phase**
It's a dynamic exchange of ideas and visions, ensuring that each design not only captures the essence of the brand but also resonates with its intended audience.

**03. Lift-off & Maintenance Phase**
The selected design is finalized and prepared for launch. Post-launch support ensures the design continues to meet the brand's needs.
